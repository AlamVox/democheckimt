Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible to apply for IMTSAT 2026? +

Currently, students studying in Class VIII, IX and X are fully eligible to apply for the IMTSAT 2026 assessment. They must be enrolled in a recognized school.

Is there any negative marking in the exam? +

No, there is absolutely no negative marking in the IMTSAT 2026. Students are encouraged to attempt all 50 questions.

How will the Top 3 Scholarships be distributed? +

Rank 1 will receive ₹10,000, Rank 2 will receive ₹6,000, and Rank 3 will receive ₹4,000. The amount will be transferred directly to the parent's or student's bank account.

About the Institute

Empowering the next generation through technology, innovation and student opportunities.

Who We Are

The Institute of Modern Technology (IMT) is a technology-driven organization committed to building meaningful impact through innovation, digital solutions, and educational initiatives.


Established with the vision of combining technology, knowledge, and opportunity, our organization operates across multiple domains including IT Services, Technical Support, Web Application Development, and Mobile Application Development, delivering modern solutions for individuals, institutions, and businesses.


Beyond technology services, we strongly believe that innovation becomes truly meaningful when it creates opportunities for people. With this purpose, we actively conduct educational and student-focused initiatives that encourage young learners to explore the possibilities of the modern digital world.


One of our key initiatives is our Scholarship and Student Awareness Program, created to identify potential, support academic growth, and inspire students to engage with emerging fields such as Artificial Intelligence (AI), technology, digital innovation, and future-ready skills.


Our scholarship initiatives are designed not only to provide financial support and recognition but also to create awareness among students about how education and technology together can shape stronger opportunities for the future.


At Institute of Modern Technology (IMT), we believe education should go beyond traditional boundaries. Students should be encouraged to think creatively, adapt confidently, and develop the mindset required to succeed in a rapidly evolving world.


Through assessments, awareness activities, educational campaigns, and technology-driven initiatives, we aim to contribute toward building a more informed, innovative, and opportunity-driven generation.

"To create an ecosystem where technology, education, and opportunity work together to empower every student and inspire innovation for a better future."

🚀 Our Mission

English 10 2 20
