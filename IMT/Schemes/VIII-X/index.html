Frequently Asked Questions

What is IMTSAT? +

IMTSAT (Institute of Modern Technology Scholarship Assessment Test) is a scholarship examination designed to identify and reward talented students through academic assessment and merit-based scholarships.

Is there any age limit? +

No. There is no specific age limit. Eligibility is determined based on the student's current class.

Which educational boards are eligible? +

Students from all recognized educational boards in India are eligible, including:

  • CBSE
  • ICSE / ISC
  • State Boards
  • Madrasa Boards
  • Other recognized boards

Is there any minimum percentage requirement? +

Students must have successfully passed their previous academic class. No specific minimum percentage is required unless announced otherwise.

How can I register for IMTSAT? +

You can register online by completing the application form and paying the registration fee through the official website.

What is the registration fee? +

The registration fee is mentioned on the official registration page and may vary depending on the examination category.

How will I receive my admit card? +

Admit cards will be available for download from the official website before the examination date.

What is the examination mode? +

The examination mode (Offline/Online) will be clearly mentioned in the official notification and admit card.

What subjects will be included in the exam? +

The examination generally includes questions from:

  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Logical Reasoning
  • General Knowledge
  • English

How are scholarships awarded? +

Scholarships are awarded based on examination performance and rank secured in IMTSAT.

What prizes will top performers receive? +

Top-ranking students may receive:

  • Rank Certificates
  • Participation Certificates
  • Cash Awards
  • Educational Gifts and Recognition
As per the scholarship structure announced for the respective year.

Will every participant receive a certificate? +

Yes. Every student who appears for the examination will receive a Participation Certificate.

How can I check my eligibility? +

You can use the "Check Your Eligibility" tool available on the website before submitting your application.

Can I edit my application after submission? +

Generally, submitted applications cannot be edited. Please review all information carefully before final submission.

How can I contact IMTSAT for support? +

You can contact the IMTSAT Help Desk through:

  • Email Support
  • Phone Call Support
  • Official Website Contact Form

What happens if I provide incorrect information? +

Applications containing false or misleading information may be rejected, and scholarship benefits may be withdrawn.

Where will the examination be conducted? +

The examination will be conducted at designated schools, colleges, or examination centers announced by IMTSAT before the exam date.

Can students from any state of India apply? +

Yes. Students from any state or union territory of India may apply if they satisfy the eligibility criteria.

Is IMTSAT affiliated with any government organization? +

IMTSAT is an independent scholarship assessment initiative. Any affiliations, partnerships, or recognitions will be mentioned officially on the website.

Who We Are

The Institute of Modern Technology (IMT) is a technology-driven organization committed to building meaningful impact through innovation, digital solutions, and educational initiatives.

IMTSAT 2026

Information Bulletin

(Institute of Modern Technology – Scholarship Assessment Test)

1. Introduction

The Institute of Modern Technology () conducts the IMTSAT 2026 to identify talented students and award scholarships based on academic performance. The exam encourages students to excel and supports them financially.

2. Eligibility Criteria

  • Students of Class VIII to X are eligible.
  • Must be enrolled in a recognized school.

3. Examination Details

  • Mode: Offline (Pen-Paper Based)
  • Duration: 1 Hours (60 Minutes)
  • Exam Time: 12:00 PM - 01:00 PM
  • Reporting: 10:00 AM – 11:30 AM
  • Gate Closing: 11:30 AM (No entry after this)

4. Exam Pattern & Marking Scheme

Subject Questions Marks per Q. Total Marks
English 10 2 20
Mathematics 15 2 30
Reasoning 15 2 30
General Knowledge 10 2 20
Grand Total 50 — 100

❌ No Negative Marking

5. Detailed Syllabus

📖 English

  • Reading Comprehension

    Unseen passages (factual, literary, discursive), Vocabulary in context (synonyms, antonyms), Inference-based questions, Theme / central idea etc.

  • Grammar & Usage

    Parts of speech, Tenses (basic to intermediate), Active & passive voice, Direct & indirect speech, Articles & determiners, Prepositions, Conjunctions, Sentence transformation etc.

  • Vocabulary

    Synonyms & antonyms, Idioms & phrases, One-word substitutions, Word usage in sentences etc.

➗ Mathematics

  • Number System

    Knowing Our Numbers, Whole Numbers, Playing with Numbers, Factors and Multiples, Prime and Composite Numbers, Even and Odd Numbers, Divisibility Rules, Roman Numerals etc.

  • Arithmetic Operations

    Addition and Subtraction of Whole Numbers, Multiplication of Whole Numbers, Division of Whole Numbers, Properties of Numbers (commutative, associative, distributive) etc.

  • Fractions & Decimals

    Fractions (basics and types), Equivalent Fractions, Simplification of Fractions, Addition and Subtraction of Fractions, Decimals etc.

  • Geometry

    Basic Geometrical Ideas (point, line, ray, segment), Angles (types and measurement), Triangles and Quadrilaterals, Circles, Symmetry, Construction of Basic Shapes etc.

  • Data Handling & Mensuration

    Data Handling (pictographs, bar graphs), Perimeter, Area etc.

  • Algebra & Patterns

    Introduction to Algebra, Simple Expressions, Patterns and Sequences etc.

🧠 Reasoning

  • Basic Logical Reasoning

    Coding-Decoding, Blood Relation, Direction Sence Test, Analogy, Classification (Odd One Out), Alphabet Series, Number Series, Missing Characters

  • Puzzle & Arrangement

    Linear Seating Arrangement, Circular Seating Arrangement, Square/Rectangular Arrangement, Floor-Based Puzzle, Box-Based Puzzle, Scheduling Puzzle Tabulation Puzzle

  • Visual & Spatial Reasoning

    Cubes and Dice, Paper Folding, Paper Cutting, Mirror Images, Water Images, Embedded Figures, Figure Counting

  • Analytical Reasoning

    Syllogism, Statement and Conclusion, Statement and Assumptions, Statement and Arguments, Inference, Data Sufficiency, Decision Making

🌍 General Knowledge / General Studies

  • History

    Ancient, Medieval, and Modern History Indian Freedom Movement etc.

  • Geography

    Physical Geography (mountains, rivers, climate, Dam etc.,), Indian Geography (states, resources, agriculture etc,)

  • Polity

    Constitution of India (Basic), Fundamental Rights(Basic) etc.

    • General Science

    Physics (motion, energy, laws), Chemistry (reactions, elements), Biology (human body, plants, animals) etc.

  • Current Affairs

    National & International News, Government schemes Sports, Awards, Appointments, Science & Technology updates etc.

  • Culture & Miscellaneous

    Indian Culture (dance, music, festivals), Books & Authors Important Days, Awards & Honors

  • Static GK (General Awareness)

    Capitals & Countries, Important Organizations (UN, WHO),Famous Personalities, National Symbols etc.
